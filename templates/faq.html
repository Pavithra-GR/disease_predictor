FAQs

How accurate is the disease prediction?

Our predictions are based on a trained KNN model and TF-IDF vectorization. While we strive for accuracy, results may vary. Always consult a doctor for a definitive diagnosis.

What symptoms should I enter?

Enter all relevant symptoms you're experiencing, such as fever, cough, or fatigue. Be as specific as possible for better predictions. For example, instead of "I feel bad," describe "I have a high fever, chills, and a sore throat."

Is my data safe?

We do not store your symptom data after your session ends. However, we recommend reviewing our Privacy Policy for more details on how your information is handled.

Can I use this app for emergency situations?

No, this app is not designed for emergencies. If you’re experiencing a medical emergency, please contact your local emergency services immediately.

How does the app handle my symptom data?

Your symptom data is processed in real-time to generate predictions and is not saved on our servers. We prioritize your privacy and do not share your data with third parties.

What if my symptoms don’t match any diseases?

If your symptoms don’t match any diseases in our database, the app may suggest consulting a doctor. Our model is trained on a limited set of diseases, so it may not cover all possible conditions.

Can I use the app for children or pets?

The app is designed for adult human use and may not provide accurate predictions for children or pets. Please consult a pediatrician or veterinarian for appropriate advice.
